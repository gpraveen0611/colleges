About Gargi College, Delhi University
Gargi College is on Siri Fort Road in New Delhi. It is a women’s college affiliated to Delhi University and is one of the leading South Campus Colleges under Delhi University. The public college founded in 1967 offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes under different streams like arts, commerce as well as science and has separate departments for the courses. Gargi College is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with ‘A’ grade. The institute is also approved by University Grants Commission (UGC) and All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE). Among other rankings, Gargi College is ranked at 16th place among colleges by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) India 2020.
